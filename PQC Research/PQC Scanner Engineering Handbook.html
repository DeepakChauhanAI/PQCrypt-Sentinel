1. Project Goals

The purpose of a PQC scanner is to discover cryptographic assets, identify quantum-vulnerable algorithms, map dependencies, prioritize remediation, and support migration to post-quantum cryptography.

Primary objectives:

  • Enterprise-wide cryptographic visibility
  • Asset inventory creation
  • Certificate discovery
  • Algorithm inventory
  • Risk scoring
  • PQC readiness assessment
  • Migration planning

4. Cryptography Fundamentals

Symmetric Encryption

AES and ChaCha20 use the same key for encryption and decryption.

Asymmetric Encryption

RSA and ECC use public/private key pairs.

Hashing

SHA-256, SHA-384, SHA-512 and SHA-3.

Digital Signatures

Provide integrity and authentication.

Key Exchange

RSA, Diffie-Hellman, ECDH and future PQ KEMs.
